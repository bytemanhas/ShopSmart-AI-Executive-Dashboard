# ShopSmart-AI-Executive-Dashboard
AI-powered executive intelligence dashboard built in Power BI featuring KPI monitoring, AI insights engine, risk analysis, and strategic decision simulation.

Project Overview

AI Executive Decision Copilot is an advanced multi-page Power BI dashboard designed to help business leaders monitor performance, identify risks, analyze root causes, and simulate strategic decisions using AI-driven insights.

This dashboard transforms raw business data into actionable executive intelligence through KPI tracking, AI insights, risk assessment, and scenario planning.

Business Problem

Modern businesses generate large volumes of data across revenue, sales, marketing, and customer operations.

However, executives often struggle with:

Lack of centralized performance visibility
Delayed identification of business risks
Difficulty understanding root causes
Limited decision simulation capability

This project solves these challenges by combining analytics with AI-inspired business intelligence.
